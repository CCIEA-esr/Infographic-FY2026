The ichthyoplankton assemblage from spring CalCOFI larval fish surveys in 2024 was similar to assemblages in 2017-23, and continued to reflect elevated abundances of larval rockfishes, larval anchovy, northern lampfish, and southern mesopelagics. Larval abundances of Pacific hake, Pacific mackerel and jack mackerel were also very high in 2024, while larval hake abundance was the highest in the time series.


Mean abundance (ln(x+1)) index of the larvae of key forage species in the Southern CCE, from spring CalCOFI surveys during 1997 - 2024 (no data from 2020). The blue shaded area is the most recent 5 years of data. Arrows indicate if the recent 5-year trend is positive (↗), neutral (→), or negative (↗). Symbols indicate if the recent 5-year mean is above the upper blue line (+), within the blue lines (↗), or below the lower blue line (−).

The outlook for snowpack in 2026 is limited to cumulative snowpack through January 31, an imperfect predictor of SWE in April. Thus far, 2026 snowpack levels are exceptionally low (<50% NRCS 1991-2020 median) throughout most of the west, except for northern Washington, central California, and central Idaho.

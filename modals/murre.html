In 2025, seabird indicators reflected generally favorable conditions for breeding in the Central and Northern CCE, with most species recovering from relatively lower productivity in 2024. Late winter upwelling may have pre-conditioned the system, through early productivity, to facilitate higher spring reproductive success.

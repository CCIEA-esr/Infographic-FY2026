Anomalies of April 1st snow-water equivalent (SWE) in the southern freshwater ecoregions of the CCE, 1990-2025. Error envelopes represent 95% credible intervals. Snow-water equivalent data were derived from the California Department of Water Resources snow survey (http://cdec.water.ca.gov/) and the Natural Resources Conservation Service’s SNOTEL sites in OR and CA.

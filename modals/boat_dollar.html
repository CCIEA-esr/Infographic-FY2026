Total revenue in commercial fisheries coastwide increased in 2025 by 6% from 2024, largely driven by increases in revenue from HMS, Pacific whiting, non-whiting groundfish, and market squid fisheries.


Annual coastwide revenue (ex-vessel value in 2025 dollars) from West Coast commercial fisheries (data from PacFin) from 1981-2025. The blue shaded area is the most recent 5 years of data. Arrows indicate if the recent 5-year trend is positive (↗), neutral (→), or negative (↗). Symbols indicate if the recent 5-year mean is above the upper blue line (+), within the blue lines (↗), or below the lower blue line (−). Whiting revenue includes shoreside and at-sea values from PacFIN, NORPAC (North Pacific Groundfish Observer Program) and NMFS Office of Science & Technology.

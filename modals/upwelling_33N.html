Overall, 2025 saw average to above-average levels of total integrated upwelling within the Southern California Bight (39°N). This year were several pulses of upwelling during January and February, before a period of above-average upwelling during most of the summer. The fall transition to a mostly downwelling state occurred in late August/early September.

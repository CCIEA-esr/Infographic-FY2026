Overall, 2024 saw average levels of total cumulative upwelling in the north (Newport, OR; 45°N). The reduction in total upwelling was related to a delay in the upwelling season due to the influence of El Niño. During the summer, there were also several periods of significant upwelling relaxation, or even downwelling.
